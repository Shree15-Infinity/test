About Us

The Vayu, often referred to as a smart ventilator or bag valve mask (BVM), is a hand-held device commonly used to provide positive pressure ventilation to individuals who are not breathing or not breathing adequately. It is widely used in emergency situations, such as cardiac arrest, respiratory failure, or during anesthesia to assist or replace spontaneous breathing.
